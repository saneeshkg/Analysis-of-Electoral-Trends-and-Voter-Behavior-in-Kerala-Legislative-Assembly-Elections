# Analysis of Electoral Trends and Voter Behavior in Kerala Legislative Assembly Elections


## Overview
This project analyzes Kerala legislative assembly election data to understand voting patterns, party dominance, and competition across constituencies.

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains candidate-level election results, including:
- Year  
- Assembly Constituency Name  
- Candidate Name  
- Party  
- Margin  
- Vote Share Percentage  
- Turnout Percentage  

Only general election years (2011, 2016, 2021) are considered.  
By-election results are excluded to maintain consistency.

---

## Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Plotly  

---

## Visualizations

### 1. Competition Analysis

#### Closest Elections (2021)
Shows constituencies with the smallest winning margins, indicating highly competitive seats.

---

### 2. Party Performance

#### Party Wins (Year-wise)
Compares top parties based on seats won across different election years.

#### Average Margin by Party
Shows average winning margin of major parties, indicating strength of victories.

---

### 3. Relationship Analysis

#### Turnout vs Margin 
Analyzes the relationship between voter turnout and winning margin.

---

### 4. Distribution Analysis

#### Margin Distribution
Shows how winning margins are distributed across constituencies.

#### Candidates per Constituency
Displays the number of candidates contesting in each constituency, reflecting competition levels.

---

### 5. Demographic Analysis

#### Gender of Winners
Shows distribution of winners by gender.

#### Age Distribution
Displays the age distribution of winning candidates.

---

### 6. Additional Insights

#### Vote Share Distribution 
Shows variation in vote share among winning candidates.

---

## Key Insights
- Elections show moderate competition with several close contests.  
- A few parties consistently dominate across years.  
- Turnout has limited impact on winning margins.  
- Competition varies across constituencies.
